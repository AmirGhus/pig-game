How this game works

In your turn you have to roll dices as much as you want. and you can save your number by clicking the hold button, then your turn will pass to your opponent, he will roll some dices too then he can hold his number and add to his score just like you. The player who reach 100 will be the winner.

But there is a rule :

If you get One, your current number will be 0 and now your opponent will roll some dices, So you can not add anything to your score.
